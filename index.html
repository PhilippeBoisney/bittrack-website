BitTrack

BitTrack is a Bitcoin-focused portfolio tracking mobile app (android so far) designed to provide clear, consistent, and accurate performance analysis. It helps users monitor purchases and sales over time, apply FIFO-based calculations, account for transaction fees, and evaluate both realized and unrealized gains. BitTrack is built for long-term investors who want a transparent view of their Bitcoin strategy, including DCA tracking and precise performance metrics.
