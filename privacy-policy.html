Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is a priority for BitTrack.

This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the application.

1. Data collected

BitTrack collects only the data necessary for the proper functioning of the application, including:

  • data voluntarily entered by the user (transactions, preferences),
  • subscription and in-app purchase related data handled by third-party services,
  • anonymous technical data required for application stability and improvement.

No unnecessary or excessive data is collected.

2. Data security

User data is stored and transmitted securely.

Technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

However, no technical solution can guarantee absolute security.

3. Data sharing

BitTrack does not sell, rent, or share personal data for commercial purposes.

Certain data may be processed by third-party providers strictly necessary for the operation of the application, including:

  • payment and subscription management services (RevenueCat, Google Play, App Store),
  • application diagnostics and crash reporting services (Crashlytics).

These providers are subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Payment data

Payment information is never stored by BitTrack.

Payments and subscriptions are handled exclusively by the distribution platforms (Google Play or App Store).

5. User rights

In accordance with applicable regulations, users have the right to:

  • access their data,
  • correct their data,
  • delete their account and associated data.

These actions can be performed directly from the application.

6. Data retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service.

In the event of account deletion, associated data is deleted within a reasonable timeframe, unless legal obligations require otherwise.

7. Changes to the policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time.

Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding privacy or personal data management, users may contact the developer through the contact methods provided in the application.

BitTrack

BitTrack is a Bitcoin-focused portfolio tracking mobile app (android so far) designed to provide clear, consistent, and accurate performance analysis. It helps users monitor purchases and sales over time, apply FIFO-based calculations, account for transaction fees, and evaluate both realized and unrealized gains. BitTrack is built for long-term investors who want a transparent view of their Bitcoin strategy, including DCA tracking and precise performance metrics.
